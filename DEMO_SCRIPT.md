## RECORDING TIPS

1. **Clear, confident narration** - Practice before recording
2. **Smooth transitions** - Plan mouse movements
3. **Show real data** - Use actual researched content
4. **Highlight AI moments** - Pause slightly when AI is working
5. **End strong** - The tagline is memorable
